A HISTORY OF QUALITY

Founded in 1932, a group of extreme cross-coutry skiers who explored through the Swiss Alps decided to provide others with the same much-loved experiences the group encountered on their journeys.

The group established their first resort in St. Moritz, Switzerland in the heart of the Alps. They focused and rooted this resort in high-quality slopes, food and lodging. As a result of thier vast knowledge of European mountain ranges, they expanded to areas that could foster similar experiences. The brand has now developed to be globally regarded as one of the best skiing and lodging provisionaries in the world.

Just over a year after the introduction of the Euro (€), EuroSki now had 5 resorts across 3 countries, all with ✩✩✩✩✩ ratings. EuroSki decided to go public and adopted the tag €ski which has become the moniker for the brand.
